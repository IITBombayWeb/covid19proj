Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I rely on the accuracy of the predictions?

    1. As with any statistical model, here too we cannot assure exactness of the prediction. The model is more accurate in places having more than 10 deceased, and that have followed similar interventions used in most countries. The projections for the following week is more accurate than further in the future. The model continuously revises the predictions as fresh data becomes available. The historical performance of the model will soon be available for viewing here. Please write to ansumali at jncasr . ac . in for technical clarifications on the model.

  2. I think the inventory estimate for item XYZ is wrong or I want to see the estimate of item ABC. Whom to contact?

    1. Please provide some justifications, along with your professional experience and affiliation in an email to p.sunthar at iitb . ac . in

  3. I know that my district has more than N positive cases. Still, there are no projections, why?

    1. For several confirmed cases, the data available publicly do not have a district associated with them. Please write to us with the details of the number and the dates they were discovered. We will update the predictions after verification from reliable sources.

  4. Why is the current date older than today's date?

    1. The data for a day is usually confirmed by the end of the day. Therefore the "current" date is set to yesterday.

  5. How do I obtain district level predictions

    1. Use keyboard to select the state and district from the drop down. OR

    2. Use the India map on the left to zoom into the state and district.

    3. Hover the mouse on the district to see the name.

    4. Click on the chosen district.

    5. The data for the inventory will appear on the right.

    6. On the top, choose the Week in future to get the projected numbers for that week.

  6. How do I obtain a state and India level data?

    1. Click on any district of the state.

    2. The data for that district and the state and national level will be displayed in the inventory table.
