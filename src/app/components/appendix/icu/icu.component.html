Requirements for a 10-Bed ICU

This page provides the requirements for a 10-bed ICU that can cater to COVID-19 patients requiring mechanical ventillation support. They are classified under the heads of:

  • Staffing
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
  • Infrastructure
  • Equipment
  • Drugs
  • Expendables
  • Oxygen related items
  • Linen
  • Pantry and Miscellaneous

Infrastructure

Item Measure Explanation
Large Room 20 m X 11 m 225 sqft space required for 1 patient in open ICU, 250 sq feet if ICU has a cubicle format. 10 patients need 2250 sqft area (209 sq mts).
Airconditioning Hospital requirement of airconditioning is 1 ton AC for every 150-250 sqft. So for an area of 2550 sft we need about 10 ton Airconditioners, with at least 12 airchanges per hour and possibly 50% fresh air change.
Generator backup
Medical gas pipeline 2 oxygen, 1 suction, 1 compressed air at each bed

The Oxygen requirement has been calculated for the worst case scenario when all 10 patients are on ventilator and requiring high FiO2. The actual requirement may be less than this if only 50% patients are on ventilator and remaining are on oxygen support only.
