About this site

This web application provides the projected requirement for various medical inventory across districts, states and the national level. This should be helpful in planning for infrastructure, arranging essential human resources and procurement of materials.

This application was developed on an initiative of the Office of the Principal Scientific Adviser to the Government of India.

The basic projected district-wise patient data comes from mesoscalelab.github.io/covid19 , based on a model proposed by Dr. Santosh Ansumali (Engineering Mechanics Unit, JNCASR , Bengaluru) and Dr. Aloke Kumar (Mechanical Engineering, IISc , Bengaluru).

The model for the projected inventory requirement was developed by Dr. Deepak Padmanabhan (MD, DM, Cardiologist, Sri Jayadeva Institute of Cardiology, Bengaluru) and Prof. P Sunthar (Chemical Engineering, IIT Bombay, Mumbai).

The web application was conceived and managed by P Sunthar, designed and developed by Bevy Insight (thanks to: Pramila Shankar and Mayank Modi). The data API to mesoscalelab.github.io/covid19 was provided by Dr. Soumyadeep Bhattacharya (SankhyaSutra Labs Pvt. ltd), which in turn relies on covid19india.org patient database. The district level map was obtained from Guneet Narula ( @github).
